A World Unlike Any Other

Roughly 35 years ago, a catastrophic magical accident called the Shimmer Scar shattered the Veil between dimensions. Multiple worlds fused together — creating wild magic zones, impossible geography, and a sentient darkness called the Void that lurks at the edges and grows closer every day.

Queen Isolde rules the Old Realm from Highcross. Nexus Labs in the Modern Quarter studies where technology meets magic. Road-walkers roam the Eastern Reaches. Pirates rule the Shattered Seas beyond the Pale Markers. And Vyraxthion the Dragon watches from the Ember Peaks, remembering a world before civilizations.

Technology & Architecture

Built on a modern, scalable stack designed for reliability and rapid iteration.

Discord Bot

discord.py with a full slash-command architecture, cog-based modularity, and automatic shard management.

AI Backend

OpenAI GPT models with per-guild cost caps, tiered rate limiting, and an emergency kill switch.

Database

PostgreSQL for durable data (users, characters, analytics) plus Redis for ephemeral caching and rate limits.

Payments

Stripe integration handling subscriptions, one-time point purchases, gifting, and automated chargeback protection.

Hosting

Railway with Docker for zero-downtime deploys, auto-scaling, and integrated logging.

Web Server

aiohttp serving HTML pages, webhooks, health checks, and API endpoints directly from the bot process.

Analytics

Real-time command tracking, DAU/MAU stickiness, streak distribution, churn detection, and weekly owner reports.

Content System

Modular character packs with a marketplace, verification pipeline, and community upvoting.
