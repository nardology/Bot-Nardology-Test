1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal information you disclose to us

In Short: We collect personal information that you provide to us through your use of the KAI Discord bot.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you interact with the Services, participate in activities on the Services, or otherwise contact us. The personal information we collect depends on how you interact with us and the Services, the choices you make, and the features you use.

Personal Information Provided by You. The personal information we collect may include the following:

  • Discord User IDs and usernames — received from Discord as part of normal bot operation
  • Usage data — talk counts, roll counts, daily active tracking, quest progress, and other interaction analytics
  • Conversation content — messages sent to AI-powered characters within KAI
  • Moderation records — bans, reports, and appeals
  • Bond/relationship data — XP, nicknames, streaks, and other character interaction data
  • User-generated content — custom characters and content packs created by users

Sensitive Information. We do not process sensitive personal information.

Payment Data. We may collect data necessary to process your payment if you choose to make purchases. All payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card numbers, bank account details, or billing address. We only store a Stripe Customer ID linked to your Discord User ID for the purpose of managing subscriptions and purchase history.

All personal information that you provide to us must be true, complete, and accurate, and you must notify us of any changes to such personal information.

Information automatically collected

In Short: Some usage information is collected automatically when you interact with the KAI bot.

Because KAI operates exclusively as a Discord bot, the information we automatically collect is limited to your interactions with the bot within Discord. This includes:

  • Command usage data — which bot commands you use, when, and how frequently
  • Interaction timestamps — date/time stamps associated with your usage of the Services
  • Server context — the Discord server (guild) ID where interactions take place

We do not collect your IP address, browser characteristics, device information, operating system, or referring URLs. That information is held by Discord and governed by Discord's Privacy Policy.
